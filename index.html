About Me

Hello, I’m Samantha Calkiewicz, a Business Analytics and AI professional with a background that bridges healthcare, operations, and data.

I hold a Master of Science in Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence from the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley and a Bachelor of Science from Oak Point University. My work focuses on turning data into clear, actionable insights for operations, finance, and patient care.

Currently, I work in Administrative & Operations support at Pro Prevent LLC, where I build centralized systems, inventory tracking, and automated dashboards that enhance workflow efficiency, improve data accuracy, and support faster operational decisions.

Prior to transitioning into analytics, I worked as a Mammography Technologist at Advocate Aurora Good Shepherd Hospital, managing data-sensitive diagnostic workflows using Epic, PenRad, and PACS. I helped increase daily exam throughput while maintaining strict HIPAA compliance across thousands of patient records.

My foundation in business operations also comes from leading the digital transformation of A’vion Salon & Spa—implementing POS, scheduling, and financial systems that improved client satisfaction and financial performance.

Technical skill areas include: Python (pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn), R, SQL, Tableau, Power BI, Excel (PivotTables, Solver), AWS, Hive, Spark, and HIPAA-compliant data governance. I specialize in KPI development, dashboard automation, workflow optimization, forecasting, and classification modeling.
