About Me

Hello, I’m Samantha Calkiewicz, a data-driven problem solver with a strong foundation in Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence. I’m on track to graduate in December 2025 with a Master of Science in Business Analytics & Artificial Intelligence from UTRGV. Through this program, I’ve gained expertise in Python, R, SQL, Tableau, Power BI, and machine learning through projects in predictive modeling, text mining, and dashboard design.

My career spans healthcare, independent consulting, and business operations. As a Mammography and Radiologic Technologist, I thrived in high-stakes environments, improving workflows, creating KPIs, and protecting sensitive data. In analytics consulting roles, I’ve delivered measurable results, including driving 12% revenue growth, cutting cancellations by 25% ($5K monthly), and reducing reporting time by 30%.

Core skills include data visualization, predictive modeling, SQL and database management, KPI development, workflow optimization, and healthcare process improvement. I’m passionate about uncovering opportunities in data, improving efficiency, and telling stories with insights that help organizations make smarter, data-driven decisions.
