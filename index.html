About Me

Hi, I’m Samantha Calkiewicz, a data-driven problem solver with a strong foundation in Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence. With hands-on experience across Python, R, SQL, and Tableau, I specialize in turning complex datasets into actionable insights that drive smarter decisions.

I am currently completing my MS in Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence at UTRGV, graduating in December 2025. Over the past five years, I built a career in healthcare ranging from X-ray to mammography, which shaped my ability to work with precision, manage high-stakes situations, and adapt to fast-paced environments. These experiences complement my technical skills and bring a unique perspective to my work in analytics.

Core skills include data analysis and visualization (Python, R, Tableau, Power BI), machine learning and predictive modeling (scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch), text mining and natural language processing, SQL and database management, and building reports and dashboards that guide business decisions.

I am passionate about using analytics to uncover opportunities, improve efficiency, and tell stories with data that decision-makers can act on. My goal is to combine technical expertise with business knowledge to create real impact in healthcare, product, and business analytics.
