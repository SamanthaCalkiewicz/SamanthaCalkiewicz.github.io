About Me

Hi, I’m Samantha Calkiewicz, a data-driven problem solver with a strong foundation in Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence. I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and real-world experience, combining advanced analytics skills with a proven background in healthcare and business operations.

I am currently completing my Master of Science in Business Analytics & Artificial Intelligence at UTRGV (Dec 2025), where I’ve developed deep expertise in Python, R, SQL, Tableau, Power BI, and machine learning frameworks. My academic training is reinforced by hands-on projects in text mining, predictive modeling, and dashboard development.

Professionally, I’ve built a career that spans healthcare, independent analytics consulting, and business operations. As a Mammography and Radiologic Technologist, I thrived in high-stakes environments, leading process improvements, creating KPIs, and safeguarding sensitive data (HIPAA). In my roles, I’ve delivered results such as:

  • Driving 12% revenue growth through retention and scheduling efficiency insights
  • Cutting same-day cancellations by 25%, recovering about $5,000 in monthly revenue
  • Increasing exam throughput by 50% with KPI-driven workflow tracking
  • Reducing manual reporting time by 30% with automated reporting workflows
